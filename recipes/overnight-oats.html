Description

Overnight oats are a fan-favorite because of their grab-and-go nature. Sometimes you just need a quick breakfast ready to go in the morning!

They're also quite easy to customize with a wide variety of toppings.

Ingredients

  • Rolled (old-fashioned) oats
  • Soy milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Maple syrup
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Blueberries
  • Bananas
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Combine oats, milk, seeds, syrup, cinnamon, and salt in a mason jar and store in refrigerator the night before
  2. Remove from fridge when ready to eat
  3. Add blueberries, sliced banana, and peanut butter
