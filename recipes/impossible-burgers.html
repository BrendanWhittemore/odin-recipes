Description

Burgers have always been a comfort food for me, even as I began to cut animal products out of my diet.

No matter your dietary restrictions, there's a burger for you! For me, that burger is this one.

Ingredients

  • Impossible patty
  • Bun
  • Vegan cheese slice
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickle
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Toast the buns
  2. Season and cook the patty, adding cheese after flipping
  3. Place patty onto buns with desired toppings
