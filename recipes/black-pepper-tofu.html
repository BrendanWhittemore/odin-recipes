Black Pepper Tofu

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes of all time. There's nothing that reminds me of home like a large serving of spicy sauced-up tofu over a bed of white rice.

This pairs great with a side of brocolli and a garnish of basil or cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Cornstarch
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Grated ginger
  • Sliced garlic
  • Sliced shallots
  • Chopped serrano pepper
  • Sliced green onion
  • White rice
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Press tofu, slice into small cubes, and toss with cornstarch
  2. Combine soy sauce, black pepper, and ginger in a glass measuring cup to create the sauce
  3. Add sesame oil to the pan and cook tofu until golden-brown
  4. Remove tofu from pan
  5. Add more sesame oil to the pan and cook the garlic, shallots, and serrano pepper for a few minutes
  6. Add tofu back to the pan along with the sauce and green onions
  7. Toss until sauce coats the tofu
  8. Serve over rice with a basil garnish
